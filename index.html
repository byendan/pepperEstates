Your Loved Ones Will be in Hands You Can Trust

The Peppers is a California Licensed Residential Care Facility for the Elderly ( RCFE ) # 425801439, and is overseen by the California Department of Social Services, Community Care Licensing Division.

What is an RCFE?

An RCFE category is considered a non-medical board and care style home, and offers structured 'care and supervision' for 6 beds ( the typical size in California ). Care refers to assistance with tasks of daily living ( dressing, etc. ), meals and housekeeping care. Supervision means following doctors orders regarding medications and health needs, and observing for any changes to communicate in the residents condition. No nurses are present or are required in our category.

The Peppers is required to maintain the protocols of CCLD. This includes hiring quidelines, who can be present on site, cleanliness, hygiene and food handling standards as well as care procedure and recordkeeping guidelines and medication storage / management. This 'Community Care' purpose and these 'Community Care Licensing Division 'CCLD' guidelines are the essence of our category. There are more than 8,000 RCFEs in California.
